Molly Boris, Ava Wadley, Gregory Bustillo, and Eggtooth are South-Central Texas based artists engaging in contemporary motifs, figure drawings, and non traditional print within a modern context. With an avid passion for unconventional themes, the quartet is infusing the new, odd, obscure, and progressive with the absurd and the familiar. All of their works have an abstract contemporary quality, exploring modern aesthetics through craft and drawing. With a dedication to pushing progressive boundaries, they are constantly blending craftsmanship with new and challenging notions to create captivating pieces that question and inspire their worlds.
